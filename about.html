The Craftsman — Connecticut

Woodworking is…

Woodworking is what I do, and what I have been doing for over 30 years.

After apprenticing under a German master craftsman, I began a journey that has taken me from learning traditional joinery methods to launching my own business here in Connecticut.

I have experience building a number of different types of items — cabinetry, entryways, paneling, moldings, corner cupboards, shutters, and storm doors. Look through my photos to see some of my projects. You will see an attention to detail, knowledge of methods, and skill that can help make your project ideas a reality.
