Upgrade Your Entryway with Expert Craftsmanship

Keep the drafts out and efficiency in with professionally installed groove & barbed weatherstripping. Unlike peel-and-stick DIY options, our method ensures a durable, long-lasting seal with a precise, clean fit.

Why Choose Our Weatherstrip Service?

  • Precision Installation

    We use professional-grade tools to create a custom groove and barbed fit that outperforms traditional adhesive strips.

  • Fast & Reliable

    On-site installation is quick, mess-free, and built to last.

  • Enhanced Durability

    No peeling, no shifting — just a seamless, airtight seal that protects your home.

  • Aesthetic & Functional

    Our method preserves the classic look of your entryway while increasing energy efficiency.

How It Works

01 Consultation

Contact us to discuss your door's needs.

02 Precision Groove Cutting

Using our cutting tool, we create a perfect-fit groove in the door frame.

03 Barbed Weatherstrip Installation
